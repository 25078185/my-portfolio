01 — About

About Me

Hey, I'm Lwando — a 2nd-year BSc Computer Science & Mathematics student at the University of Venda, Limpopo. I discovered my love for data when I realised that behind every decision — from business strategy to everyday life — there are numbers telling a story. I became obsessed with learning how to read that story.

I'm an aspiring Data Analyst who enjoys working with numbers, patterns, and technology to uncover meaningful insights. Whether it's cleaning a messy dataset, spotting a trend, or turning raw numbers into something visual and understandable — that process genuinely excites me.

Outside of studying, I enjoy solving puzzles and logic games — which honestly is just data analysis in disguise. I'm also passionate about using tech to solve real-world African problems. Expected to graduate in 2026.

Career Goal

Become a Data Analyst, applying mathematics and computer science to help organisations make smarter, data-driven decisions.

What Drives Me

The belief that data can expose inequality, improve communities, and tell the stories that numbers alone cannot.

Based In

Limpopo, South Africa — studying at the University of Venda, one of South Africa's rising tech institutions.

Currently Learning

Python for data analysis, SQL fundamentals, and building my first end-to-end data project.
